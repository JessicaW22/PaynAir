FAQ

Welcome to our help page!

Log in:

Type in your account-name and below your password. Press Enter! Yay your logged in wow

Log out:

Click on it to log out of the system

Select your journey:

Choose between the giving Airports where you want to start from and where you want to go.

Enter value.

Reserve seats:

You can choose between five numbers.

Number one => Reserve the seat

Enter row => The Airline has 10 rows where you can choose from.

Enter a seat => Our seats named after the lines from A to F

Enter your Name and choose between the options 1= “reserve a seat”, 2= “cancel a reservation”, 3= “display reservation”, 4= “save reservation” and 5= “exit”.

Cancel seats:

Enter the row and the number of the seat you want to cancel.

Your reservation will be canceled.

Save reservations:

If you choose this option, you will get the reservations as a txt file.
